About me

I'm Inji, UX designer and I learned coding at Parsons School of Design at my Typography and Interaction class. Honestly, diving into HTML and CSS made me so curious that I even coded this website and my portfolio myself.

I love reflecting on how emotions, storytelling, and intuitive experiences come through in my work. I specialize in enterprise design, learning, and gamification, aiming to enhance the lifestyles of those who use the interfaces I design. Feel free to check out my work on GitHub!
